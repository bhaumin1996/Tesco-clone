Tesco Marketplace Guarantee

When you shop on Tesco Marketplace, you're protected by our guarantee. Whether your order doesn't arrive, arrives damaged, or isn't as described — we've got you covered.

What we cover

  • Item not received: If your marketplace order doesn't arrive within the estimated delivery window, you're entitled to a full refund or replacement.
  • Item not as described: If an item significantly differs from its listing, raise a return within 30 days for a full refund.
  • Damaged on arrival: Contact us with photos and we'll arrange a replacement or refund.
  • Seller unresponsive: If a seller doesn't respond to your return request within 72 hours, Tesco steps in to resolve it.

Returns policy

You have 30 days from delivery to raise a return on any marketplace item. Raise your return from My Marketplace Orders. The seller will respond within 72 hours. If the seller disputes your return, Tesco will review and make a final decision.

Dispute escalation

If a seller disputes your return and you disagree with their decision, you can escalate to Tesco. Our team will review the case within 5 working days and issue a final decision. In most cases, the outcome favours the customer.

Clubcard points on Marketplace orders

You earn 1 Clubcard point per £1 spent on all eligible Tesco Marketplace purchases — the same as shopping directly with Tesco. Points are credited to your Clubcard account once the order is marked as delivered.

Seller standards

All Tesco Marketplace sellers are verified before they can list products. We monitor seller performance including on-time delivery rate, return rate, and customer ratings. Sellers who fall below our quality threshold are reviewed and may be suspended.
