Part A – Purchasing from Tesco

Prices and VAT

Please note that except as set out below the prices are guide prices only. The actual price you pay will be the price charged in-store at the time your order is picked for delivery. The actual order value cannot be determined until the day of delivery because the prices stated on the website and app may vary either above or below the prices in-store on the day your order is picked and delivered.

Please note that once you place an order for F&F clothing, you won't be able to amend that portion of your order.

When your order is delivered you may return any item and receive a full refund if you are unhappy with the price charged or for any other reason.

All prices are expressed inclusive of any VAT payable unless otherwise stated. The price of the items does not include the delivery charge which will be charged at the rate specified when you place your order.

Certain promotions available in stores may not be available online, and there may be promotions available online that you won’t be able to find in-store.

Price and Clubcard Plus promotions are available only in-store. Online groceries, Whoosh, F&F Clothing, and Marketplace are excluded.

Availability

If for any reason beyond our reasonable control, we are unable to supply a particular item, we will not be liable to you. When an item you have ordered for grocery home delivery, Click+Collect or Whoosh is unavailable we will attempt to deliver a suitable substitute, unless you have asked us not to.

To ensure availability of all our products customers may be limited to a maximum number of items.

Acceptance

Your order is an offer to buy from us. A contract is only formed when we have despatched your order. At any point up until then, we may decline to supply the goods to you.

Tesco products are available for non-commercial and domestic use only. We reserve the right to refuse or cancel orders from businesses or that we consider are for commercial or other non-domestic concerns. Such orders may also result in cancellation of applicable Clubcard membership or points, in accordance with our Clubcard Terms and Conditions.

Minimum basket charge

A minimum basket charge applies to grocery home delivery, Whoosh and Click+Collect. Please refer to the Whoosh section of this Part A below for further details.

For groceries home delivery and Click+Collect, if the value of the products in your basket when you confirm your order is less than the minimum basket value, then we will add a £5 charge to your order. We will add the charge to your total at the checkout page, before you pay.

Grocery Home Delivery £50 minimum
Click+Collect £25 minimum

There is no minimum basket value for F&F clothing purchases and F&F clothing items are not counted for the purposes of calculating the minimum basket charge for grocery home delivery and Click+Collect.

Delivery

Delivery will be made to the address specified by you when you place your order.

For deliveries to a residential apartment block, we will generally deliver to the front door of your apartment. However, we reserve the right to deliver only to the main entrance of the property if the driver believes it is unsafe or not practical to deliver to your apartment front door.

Tesco follows a "Think 25" policy when delivering age-restricted items. If the person receiving the goods looks under 25, proof of age will be requested.

Delivery and Click+Collect order items will not be packed in carrier bags. For certain products, such as packaged raw meat, we’ll still need to use red hygiene bags.

F&F clothing orders are not available for Click+Collect and will incur delivery fees. Delivery Saver does not apply to F&F clothing deliveries. Standard delivery: 3-5 working days. Next-day delivery: 1-2 working days.

Click & Collect

If you choose to collect your order from a store, the time slot and store for collection will be agreed with you when you place your order. In select stores, same day Click+Collect is available, charges apply.

Please bring your order number and the credit card you used to pay for your order. We will not be able to give you your order unless you produce an appropriate form of I.D.

Returns and Refunds

We'd like you to be happy with everything you purchase from Tesco. Please see our Returns and Refunds Policy for details of how to obtain a replacement or refund.

F&F Clothing returns: Orders returned via courier will incur a fee of £2.50, deducted from your refund. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have original tags attached.

Whoosh (Rapid Delivery)

Whoosh is only available in selected locations. The product range may differ from Tesco’s standard grocery home shopping range.

Minimum Basket Value: £15 for all Whoosh orders. Orders below this will incur an additional £2.50 minimum basket charge on top of the delivery fee.

Cancellation: You cannot make changes to any of your current orders once your order has been placed.

90-Minute Guarantee: If the delivery takes longer than 90 minutes to arrive, it will be cancelled and you will be automatically refunded.

Part B – Purchasing from Marketplace sellers

Marketplace

Tesco provides a platform for sellers to sell their products to customers. When you purchase a product on Marketplace, you enter into a contract with the seller.

Our Tesco Product Terms (Part A) do not apply when you purchase a product from a seller.

Marketplace Payment

Transactions are paid in GBP via Visa, Mastercard, Maestro, Visa Debit. American Express is not accepted for Marketplace orders.

Tesco gift cards, e-Coupons, and vouchers cannot be used to purchase products on Marketplace.

Clubcard Points

You will earn Clubcard points on Marketplace purchases at the same rate as Tesco products. However, you will not be able to spend Clubcard points on Marketplace products.

Marketplace Returns & Refunds

Returns and refunds are solely issued and dealt with by the sellers. Please do not send returns of Marketplace products to Tesco supermarkets.

You are entitled to a full refund from the seller for up to 30 days if you change your mind or your product(s) are faulty.
